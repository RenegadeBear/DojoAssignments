Doods are the most adorable muts on the planet! A 'dood' is a fun name for any dog that has been cross bred with a poodle. Strict adherents to the American Kennel Club (AKC) breeding standards consider us muts, but many have fallen in love with poodle mixes. When bred properly we are:

  • incredibly intelligent
  • great temperament
  • fun loving
  • hypoallergenic
